How to request your Spotify Data

Follow these steps:

  1. Go to the official Spotify Privacy Settings page using the button below.
  2. Log into your Spotify Account if prompted.
  3. Scroll down to the "Download your data" section.
  4. Make sure to request the Extended Streaming History (this contains your full history since account creation, not just the last year).
  5. Click "Request data". Spotify will send you an authentication email to validate your identity.
  6. Once confirmed, Spotify takes a few days to prepare your archive. You will receive an email containing a link to download a .zip file.
